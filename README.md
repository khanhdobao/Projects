# **Project Overview**

### **Objective**

The project aimed to identify the **primary drivers of revenue** and **customer dissatisfaction** within Olist’s e-commerce ecosystem. Specifically, I applied the Pareto Principle (80/20 Rule) to isolate high-value product categories and investigated how delivery performance across different Brazilian states correlates with customer review scores.

### **Context & Business Question**

Operating in the competitive Brazilian marketplace sector, Olist generates revenue through **commission fees** on seller sales. Using a dataset of ~112,000 orders, I conducted this analysis to provide actionable insights into where Olist should prioritize its **operational spending** to maximize customer retention and profitability.

In this project, I will focus on answering 2 key questions:

**1. Revenue concentration:** Which 20% of product categories generate 80% of the total revenue?

**2. Logistics & Satisfaction:** How do delivery times across different Brazilian states correlate with customer review scores?
